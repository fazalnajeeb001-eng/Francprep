## Master Curriculum Blueprint — Complete Skeleton, A1 → C2

### Full Module / Unit / Chapter / Lesson Structure
*Internal document. Governed by the FrancPrep Constitution, Editorial Style Guide, and Phase 1 Architecture. Titles and structural tags only — no lesson content, exercises, dialogues, or vocabulary lists appear below.*

---

## HOW TO READ THIS DOCUMENT

Each level below follows the fixed hierarchy established in Phase 1: **Module → Unit → Chapter → Lesson.**

Each **Chapter** entry lists: its communicative objective, its grammar focus, its vocabulary domain, and its full lesson table. Each **Lesson** row shows: lesson number within the chapter, title, and **anchor skill** (R = Reading, W = Writing, L = Listening, S = Speaking, INT = Integrated Skills, REV = Chapter Review & Mini-Assessment) — per the Constitution's rule that every lesson, regardless of anchor, exercises all four skills around shared content.

Chapter lesson counts follow Phase 1 exactly: **A1 & A2 = 8 lessons/chapter (6 skill-anchored + 1 Integrated + 1 Review)**; **B1 & B2 = 7 lessons/chapter (5 skill-anchored + 1 Integrated + 1 Review)**; **C1 = 7 lessons/chapter**; **C2 = 6 lessons/chapter (4 skill-anchored + 1 Integrated + 1 Review)**.
